# Mudoran (HLS)
## Etymology & Descendants
- **Link to the Past:** Mudora, from the ムドラの<ruby>書<rt>しょ</rt></ruby> "*Book of Mudora,*" which is the guide to translating the Ancient Hylian language known as *Mudoran*. This is the namesake of the Mudoran language developed by the HLS.

## Pronunciation
- **IPA Broad**: /m.t.r₂/

## Class 1

### mudor₂a (form inflected-lexeme)
1. knowledge 
2. (context) sense
