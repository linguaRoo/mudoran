# Mudoran (HLS)

## Etymology & Descendants
This template is constructed from the name of Din and uses initial stress, and refers to concrete things which are stable or secure. Compare to [1i2.ˈ3aː](lexicon/template/1i2.'3ā) which uses initial stress and refers to stable or secure things.

## Pronunciation
- **IPA Broad**: /ˈ1i2.3a/

## Noun Template

### ˈ1i2.3a (Din-class concrete singular)
1. a material element of the world of the Din class, usually something solid, hard, or durable
	1. **_tinr₂a_** metal, ore
	2. **_nir₃r₂a_** crystal, gemstone
	3. **_firʃa_** root, seed
	4. **_hir₃r₂a_** body, appearance
2. a created object with qualities of persistence or durability
	1. **_r₃inka_** altar
	2. **_kir₂na_** mine, tunnel
	3. **_kisʔa_** temple, cloister
	4. **_sitr₂a_** grave, graveyard, cemetery
