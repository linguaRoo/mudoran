# **_1i2.ˈ3aː_**

# Mudoran (HLS)

## Etymology & Descendants
This root is constructed from the name of Din (ディン "dʰin"), uses final stress, and refers to dangerous or powerful things. Confer with ['1i2.3a](lexicon/templates/'1i2.3a) which uses initial stress and refers to stable or secure things.

## Pronunciation
- **IPA Broad**: /1i2.ˈ3aː/

## Noun Template

	1. **_[tinr₂aː](lexicon/words/t/tin•ā)_** fire, heat
	2. **_[nir₃r₂aː](lexicon/words/n/nir₃•ā)_** ice, cold
